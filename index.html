Services

What's included

Every service is built around your specific story, experiences, and goals — not a formula.

✍️

Personal statement

In-depth intake call, original full draft written from scratch, narrative strategy, and two rounds of revisions. Final proofread with AMCAS character count check.

📋

Work & activities section

Full write-ups for up to 15 entries including Most Meaningful designations. Strategic framing of each experience to highlight growth, impact, and fit.

🏫

Secondary essays

School-specific research and original drafts for up to 10 schools (2–4 prompts each). Essays are written to complement — not repeat — your personal statement.

School list strategy

Curated list of up to 30 schools balanced across reach, target, and safety. School-by-school rationale covering fit and realistic acceptance likelihood.

Interview prep

Mock sessions in traditional or MMI format with written feedback. Coaching on delivery, body language, and common scenarios like ethics and adversity questions.

Strategy session

Included with every package. We start with your full picture — GPA, MCAT, experiences, goals — before a single word gets written.
